Get Fuel through USSD or App solution to make your journey easy.

Our emergency fuel service app offers a convenient solution for individuals who run out of fuel while on the road. With just a few taps on your smartphone, you can request for fuel topup, ensuring you never get stranded without fuel again.

We provide two types of fuel, that is PETROL and DIESEL. All these are available on our esteemed merchants' fuel stations namely Total, Stabex, Hass, Shell.

What makes us different!

We provide fuel to road users on a loan, and payments are made at any convinient time!

Safety and Security

Assured Safety and security of users by verifying their information and assessing the legitimacy of fuel requests.

Support all Fueling Stations

Regardless of the fueling station or brand, users can utilize the App to request fuel assistance.

Data-driven Insights

The app's transaction history and data analytics capabilities provide users with valuable insights into their fuel usage patterns.

24-7 Support

Our team is available at all times to address any issues, answer queries, and provide prompt assistance related to fuel requests & repayments.
